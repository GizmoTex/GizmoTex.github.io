Lenovo Ideapad 3i


Technical Specs

Screen Size : 15.6 inch
True Tone : No
Storage : 256GB SSD+1TB HDD/ 512GB SSD
Graphics : Nvidia GTX 1650 / Nvidia GTX 1650 Ti
RAM : 8GB/ 16GB
Keyboard : Keyboard with White backlight
Ports : 1 HDMI, 1 Charging Port, 3 USB, 1 USB-C, SD Card Slot


Review


Lenovo launched the new Ideapad 3i on 30th May 2020, with Intel's 10th Generation processors. The new ideapad is an understated, sleek gaming laptop with adequate performance on offer. The design of this laptop is simple yet feels professional enough to be carried around in offices or colleges. The metal body look of the laptop makes it stand apart from the Gamery Red chunky laptops. Spec wise, the Idepadd 3i has Intel's 10th Generation H-series processors particularly i5-10300H and i7-10750H. These are 4 and 6 core processor options. For graphics department, Lenovo has went with Nvidia's GTX 1650 lineup of graphics cards, as they are affordable and less power hungry yet delivers enough performance for an entry level gaming laptop. With 8GB of RAM, it seems less but can be upgraded upto 32GB if needed. Storage options of this laptop are adequate for most, as these come with a 256GB SSD+1TB Hard drive disk as base variant. All in all, the Ideapad 3i could be a great option for such an affordable price of $700 for base variant. Check out the Lenovo Idepad 3i in the below.
