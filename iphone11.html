Review


The younger sibling of iPhone 11 pro. It has the same specs but a lower resolution screen. But it is cheaper at $699. It has 2 back cameras rather than the 3 of the pro. But still is an amazing smartphone for couple of years usage. This iPhone 11 is the no-pro model of the latest iPhone, but the only difference is the screen and camera. Rest all the specs and performance are exactly same as the iPhone 11 pro or iPhone 11 Pro Max. Instead of the Pro's 3 camera, the iPhone has 2 cameras. And the OLED screen is replaced by an LCD screen. The design and build quality is excellent as usual but unlike the pro model, the iPhone 11 has an Aluminium-Glass build rather than the Stainless-steel -glass build of the pro. So this iPhone can be said as the affordable version of the Pro model without leaving out much of the features and thus this phone can be a compelling option for many who are looking to buy a new iPhone but want something at a cheaper rate and feels modern as well.
