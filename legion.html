Review


The Lenovo Legion lineup of laptops have been a great laptop at an affordable rate. This y540 model is the entry model of the lineup. With plastic body and mediocre specs, the y540 can deliver some decent experience to its users. The Legion is the Gaming Division of Lenovo and has a quite a few range of gaming laptops and desktops. The Legion y540 is the improvement over the previous y530. The y540 is a capable gaming laptop with an understated matte look and design. the design is different from all other laptops. The ports of this laptop are positioned behind the laptop which makes it a No-Cable-tangled laptop. The screen has some quirks too, such as it can be pushed all the way back for a flat screen look. This can have many applications while standing and using the laptop or presentation. The build quality of the laptop is good too with the soft rubber-like finished plastic body and a glowing Legion Logo. The laptop has many configurations ranging from Intel i5 with Nvidia GTX 1050 to Intel i7 with Nvidia RTX 2070 graphics. This makes the y540 able to compete in different segments.
