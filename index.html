Apple Macbook Pro 13
Apple's Pro Laptop.

Apple's ultimate laptop. It does not lose its shine this year too with its unibody metal design. The no-flex body or screen, huge precision touchpad and touchbar too, this macbook pro is still the best macbook you can get.

Apple's Affordable Laptop.

Apple's affordable laptop. It does not lose its shine this year too with its unibody metal design. The no-flex body or screen, huge precision touchpad and touch id too, this macbook air with its new butterfly keyboard is still the cheapest macbook you can get.

Apple's Ultimate Pro Laptop.

The ultimate pro laptop from Apple you can get in 2020. The 16inch almost bezel-less macbook with the highest performing processors in it makes it by far the most advanced macbook. The price matches the product too. But for an advanced piece of tech, the price is justified.



Razer Blade 15

The laptop that resembles the Macbook, in a good way. The sleek anodized aluminium unibody with great performing hardware makes it a compelling option for gamers who are looking for the understated look.
