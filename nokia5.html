Nokia 5.3 is an affordable android smartphone, at $199 te smartphone is certainly among the chepeast android smartphones out there. Even though the Nokia 5.3 is an affordable phone, it does not feel like it. As it has pretty good specs with Qualcomm's Snapdragon 665 processor and upyo 6Gb of RAM. The phone has a huge 4000MAH battery, which can quite last for 2 days depending on your use. The screen is a massive 6.55 inch HD LCD display with peak brightness of 450 nits. There are in total 5 cameras in this phone. 4 at the back and one 8 megapixels camera in front. The four cameras at the back ranges from a Wide angle lens, Ultra wide lens, a depth camera and a macro lens, which covers almost all the possible camera options except the Telephoto lens. Still with all the specs and design of this phone, the Nokia 5.3 does not feel like a cheap phone rather feels good in hand and performs even better.
