Apple Airpods
Apple's wireless earphones

The airpods are Apple's wireless solution to the music industry. Even though the cost is higher than others, the airpods still gives a flawless and seemless connection experience unlike others. The airpods pair instantly to the apple device with a flick of the case.

Apple's wireless pro earphones

These Airpods Pro are the pro alternative to the regular airpods. It has a price to match the pro tag. This one has better sound output, better clarity and noise cancellation of great quality. These pro airpods provide the seemless connectivity experience too.
