Google's Flagship Smartphone

The fastest android launched. This phone has a simple design and comes with pure stock android. The camera of this phone is class-leading with its amazing Night mode capabilities. This is one true android for the Android fans.



Samsung Galaxy S10
Samsung's Flagship Phone

The Samsung Galaxy S10, the android liked by all. The smartphone that started the boom of android market. It is now reached its 10th iteration. And it has come a far way. Flagship processor. Top end specs. Classy Design. The best Samsung phone for Team Android.

The Samsung Galaxy Note 10, S10's professional cousin. The note has always been the phone for the professionals, the people who like big phones. The advancement of S pen has made it better throughout these years.

Samsung Galaxy S20

Galaxy S20, the latest galaxy smartphone from Samsung. It has all the bells and whistles. It has three variants, S20, S20+ and S20 ultra. With this latest launch Samsung has incorporated 5G into the smartphone.

Samsung m31

Samsung launched their Affordable Android smartphone with the biggest screen and biggest battery possible. This new phone comes with a 6.4 inch screen, 600mAh battery and 6gb of RAM!!

Apple iPhone 11 Pro
Apple's Flagship Phone

Apple's best phone for offer. The $999 smartphone is still the best phone for long term. With updates ranging till 3-5 years and the physical processing capacity that easily cope up with tasks for 3-5 years is the biggest factor for any flagship iPhone. And this one does it even better.


Apple iPhone Xr
Apple's Mid-range Smartphone

Motorola Razr

Its the 2000s again. Motorola has launched its iconic Razr phone with a modern twist. The new phone comes with an all screen design, which folds in the classic Razr style and with a satisfying thud while slaming it.


Samsung Galaxy Flip
Samsung's Foldable Phone

After the not-so-successful launch of the first foldable phone, Samsung has tried again with its Galaxy Flip. This time the phone folds vertically rather than the previous one, which folds horizontally.





Motorola Edge Plus
Motorola's Flagship

Motorola has launched a new flagship phone, Edge Plus. The screen of the phone curves and bends in all sorts. The screen is stretched and folded onto the side frame of the phone, this gives it a futuristic design and brings in new features.





Lenovo k10
Lenovo's Affordable Smartphone

Lenovo has launched the K10 with an affordable price and delivers good perfomance. It is no flagship, but not all needs the latest and greatest. It has different variants that offer different storage and RAM options.


Apple iPhone 11
Entry level iPhone 11

The younger sibling of iPhone 11 pro. It has the same specs but a lower resolution screen. But it is cheaper at $699. It has 2 back cameras rather than the 3 of the pro. But still is an amazing smartphone for couple of years usage.
