Review


Dell refreshed their XPS lineup with more improvements. The desgin of this laptop has to be the greatest in the Windows laptop world. The unibody aluminium body is slim yet sturdy. This makes it a wonderful option for college use, office or on the go use. The laptop looks professional and the fit and finish is immaculate, and the look is completed with the Carbon Fibre deck. Coming to the specs of this machine. It has Intel's new 10th generation mobile processors. The 13 inch comes with a U-series low power-consuming processors, whereas the 15 and 17inch models come with an H-series processors. The laptop's base variant comes with Intel's UHD GPU, and it can be upgraded for an NVidia GTX 1650 Ti Max-Q design GPU.This package makes it a good contender for light to medium gaming usage too. Overall this laptop is a total package for someone who is looking to buy a professional looking laptop for college or office at a reasonable price. The base variant of 15inch model starts at $1299 and can be maxed out to $3000 for a 64GB, 2TB SSD i7-10875H(5.1GHZ) Model. And that model is a beast for that price.
