Review


Apple's best phone for offer. The $999 smartphone is still the best phone for long term. With updates ranging till 3-5 years and the physical processing capacity that easily cope up with tasks for 3-5 years is the biggest factor for any flagship iPhone. And this one does it even better. The iPhone 12 Pro is the successor to the iPhone 11 Pro, which was Apple's flagship of last year. This year the 12 pro is the new flagship with features supporting the name. There are 3 cameras at the back, one telephoto for potrait photos, one wide angle for regular use and one ultrawide for landscapes. The front camera is the same as previous which is present in the notch. The design of this phone has changed slightly with newer back glass style with textured surface which makes it less of a fingerprint-magnet. The overall unibody stainless steel construction still feels great and sturdy making it a durable phone. The specs of this device is off the roof as well. With Apple's A14 Bionic processor being the fastest mobile processor of all thrashes all the tasks with ease and can be relied upon for years to come. Overall, the design, the specs and the performance of the iPhone 12 pro makes it a great flagship phone, for those who can afford the $1000 bill. Check out the Apple iPhone 12 pro in the link below.
