Review


The Xbox Series X is the latest Xbox from Microsoft. The Seriex X is a slim and tall console unlike any previous Xbox. The Xbox is based on AMD's new 3rd gen Ryzen processors. The design of the new Xbox is very different from the last generation Xbox, this new Xbox has a tall boxy body with holes on top for cooling. The performance of the new Xbox is not yet measured but with the processor and graphics details, a rough estimate has been taken by many. The console is ver powerful and can supposedly run AAA titles at 4k 60fps wih Ray Tracing. The launch of the Xbox is not yet reveiled but after the intial release of the console itself, the Xbox has been quite a head turner in the Gaming industry. Well all we can do is to wait for some whil until we get hands on the new Xbox Series X.
