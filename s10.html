Review


The Samsung Galaxy S10 is the 10th iteration of the Samsung Galaxy S lineup of smartphones. The Galaxy S series was one of the first android flagships to ever launch worldwide and has been popular since then. This iteration is no different, it has an amazing AMOLED screen which has edge on both sides to make it look like a bezelless device. The specs are top-notch as well with either Qualcomm Snapdragon 855 or Exynos 9820 processor and 8GB of RAM. The storage options are limited to 128Gb and 512Gb but can be increased with a micro SD card. There are 4 cameras in this phone. 3 on the back and 1 selfie cam. The back cameras cover it all with telephoto, wide angle and ultra wide angle lenses. The Samsung s10 is still a compelling option for many even though its 2 years old. For the price of under $500 the s10 is a steal for those who are looking for an android device from a reliable name or for those who are fans of the galaxt lineup.
