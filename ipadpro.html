Review


The Pro tablet for the Pro users, Apple has launched the updated 2020 version of the iPad Pro and it is even better than the previous generation. The unibody slim styling remains the same with addition of new cameras-3 exactly and a LIDAR sensor. This year the iPad Pro focuses more on Camera and AR department. The LIDAR sensor is a new addition as it massively creates a difference in AR. The LIDAR sensor is used for measuring distances, lengths of objects and its position. Because of this and Apple's A13 Bionic processor, the iPad Pro can easily create an AR virtual world with features like creating new objects in the original world, placing furnitures and objects in real time and much more. The iPad Pro also brings in the Mouse support this year with its new accessory The Magic floating keyboard. This keyboard attachment makes the iPad Pro a futuristic laptop with a floating screen. The $999 price tag retains this year too, but it somewhat is worth for the money as there is no proper alternative to such a polished and sleek product. Apart from the price tag, there are very less cons about the iPad Pro and this makes it a great option for Creative professionals, artists and even photo-video editors.
